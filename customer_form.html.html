      1. Compliance with Regulations:
      customer agrees that all shipments comply with applicable laws and regulations, including International Air Transport Association (IATA) standards and any relevant customs, import/export regulations.

      2. Accuracy of Shipment Information:
      The customer confirms that all information provided regarding the shipment, including contents, value, and destination, is accurate and truthful. Any discrepancies may lead to the cancellation of the shipment and potential legal action.

      3. Consignee Liability:
      The customer understands that in the event of the shipment containing any illegal or restricted items, the consignee shall bear full responsibility and must face legal consequences as per local and international laws.

      4. Right to Inspect:
      Bee26 Cargo Services reserves the right to inspect any shipment to ensure compliance with all regulations. Any shipment found to contain prohibited items will be reported to the relevant authorities.

      5. Prohibited Items:
      The customer acknowledges that illegal substances, hazardous materials, and any other prohibited or restricted items are not permitted for shipping. The customer is responsible for ensuring that the contents of their shipment comply with these restrictions and Bee26 Cargo Services will not take any responsibilities.

      6. Liability for Delays or Seizure:
      Bee26 Cargo Services is not liable for any delays, losses, or seizures of shipments resulting from violations of the stated conditions or due to the involvement of law enforcement or customs authorities.

      7. Insurance Policy:
      Bee26 Cargo Services is not liable to pay any damage or loss of shipment unless the customer has accepted the Insurance Policy. Victim will get $5 compensation per each kilo and must be only the damaged items. I understand and agree that by declining insurance coverage for this shipment, I assume full responsibility for any loss or damage that may occur during transit.

      8. Estimated Delivery Time:
      Standard delivery time to Sri Lanka is approximately 10 working days. While we strive for punctuality, please be aware that unforeseen delays due to airline transit or customs clearance may occur. We appreciate your patience and professional understanding in such instances.

      9. Customs Duties & VAT Calculation:
      Customers should note that Sri Lanka Customs calculates VAT and other duties based on the assessed value of the items (CIF Method: Cost, Insurance, and Freight) and the quantity, not merely on the weight of the box. The final valuation and applicable taxes are determined solely by the Customs Officer at the time of inspection.

      10. Local Delivery Surcharges:
      The standard shipping rate covers delivery to main distribution centers. Please note that final delivery charges may vary and incur an extra fee depending on the specific delivery zone or remote location within Sri Lanka.

      IMPORTANT:

      Please be aware that Sri Lanka Customs calculates duties based on the CIF Method. You are taxed on the total cost of the shipment, not just the value of the goods.
      Taxable Total = Value of Items + Shipping Cost + Insurance Note:
      The final valuation of items is determined solely by the Customs Officer at the time of inspection.
